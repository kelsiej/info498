In the Future



How Will These Artefacts Look in 100 Years?
In 100 years, I imagine social media, in general, to look very different. I imagine that we will be accessing most of our information not through a physical screen but through augmented reality. I can envision that messages from the future pope and Dalai Lama may come as actual voice messages that we can listen to either by some device that conveys audio directly to our brain or through a hearing implant. There may even be a 3D image of them.
